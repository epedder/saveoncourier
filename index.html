How It Works

Save in just 3 simple steps

Saveoncourier delivers top shipping performance and savings to both businesses and individuals alike. Its state of the art, easy to use web interface gets your package delivered in only three simple steps.

Step 1 - Where is your package going?

Select an address from your existing contacts or enter a new one. Choose a package description or define one of your own.

Step 2 - Choose a carrier

Step 3 - Schedule pick up

Tell us the best time and place to pickup your package and you are all done. Now sit back and enjoy the savings!

About Us

Who We Are

Saveoncourier has been helping business and individuals fulfill all their shipping needs for over 15 years. We are affiliated with some of the most trusted brands in discount shipping in North America. Our consultants have helped thousands of customers find the best discounted rates from leading shipping companies like FedEx, DHL and UPS.

Exceptional Discounts

Saveoncourier offers discounts well beyond 40%, the kind of discount that only the biggest players in the market can normally expect. How do we do it? Saveoncourier holds wholesale accounts with the leading shipping companies (including FedEx, DHL and UPS), this allows us to pass the discounts to our customers. You don't need any volume commitment. Saveoncourier accounts are free for all your shipping and saving needs.

Cloud Powered Shipping

Saveoncourier uses the power of the cloud to offer a comprehensive shipping solution. Using our Shipping Calculator, our customers can compare the standard rates of leading companies -- FedEx, DHL, UPS, etc.-- with our discounted rates and make sure they always get the best price and maximize their savings. Customers can create, schedule, and track their shipments in real time with an easy to use web interface. Our tools can help small business owners, eBay and Etsy sellers and individuals get great savings and fulfill all their shipping needs from the cloud.

Extraordinary Customer Service

Saveoncourier has expert shipping consultants ready to assist you on all your discount shipping needs. Our consultants can guide you through the process of signing up, scheduling and tracking packages, all while optimizing your savings. Once your package is in transit you have direct access to customer service from the leading company delivering your package or document, whether it is FedEx, DHL or UPS, no middle man, no intermediary, all while maintaining a heavily discounted rate.
